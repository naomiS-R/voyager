Vega

Other names:

Alpha Lyrae

Facts:

  • One of the most luminous stars in the Sun's neighborhood.
  • Only 25 light-years from the Sun.
  • Vega was the northern pole star around 12,000 BC and will be so again around the year 13,727.
  • Vega was the first star other than the Sun to be photographed and the first to have its spectrum recorded.
